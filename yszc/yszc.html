本隐私政策说明 ToolKit（以下简称"本应用"）如何收集、使用、存储和保护您的个人信息。使用本应用即表示您同意本隐私政策所述内容。

📋一、我们收集的信息

本应用仅收集为提供核心功能所必需的最少信息。我们不会收集、上传或存储您的个人身份信息（如姓名、邮箱、电话号码等）。

🔧二、信息的使用方式

我们收集的信息仅用于以下目的：

  • 音视频处理：您选择的媒体文件仅在设备本地处理，处理完成后结果由您决定保存或删除
  • 免费试用管理：使用设备基本信息生成设备指纹，用于管理7次免费试用额度，防止试用次数被篡改
  • 购买验证：通过 Google Play Billing 或 Apple StoreKit 验证您的购买状态，确认高级版功能是否解锁
  • 数据安全：使用 AES-256-CBC 加密算法保护本地存储的敏感数据，防止篡改和未授权访问

🔒三、信息的存储与安全

我们高度重视您的数据安全，采取以下措施保护您的信息：

  • 本地存储：所有数据（包括使用计数、购买状态）均存储在您的设备本地，不上传至任何远程服务器
  • AES-256-CBC 加密：敏感数据（免费次数、购买状态）使用 AES-256-CBC 算法加密后存储，每次加密使用随机生成的 IV
  • 设备指纹密钥绑定：加密密钥由设备硬件指纹派生（SHA-256），与设备绑定，无法在其他设备上解密
  • HMAC-SHA256 完整性校验：加密数据附带完整性校验码，可检测任何篡改行为，篡改后数据将被重置而非误判
  • 临时文件清理：音视频处理产生的临时文件存储在系统临时目录，可由您手动清理

🤝四、信息的共享与披露

我们不会将您的个人信息出售、交易或转让给任何第三方。以下情况除外：

  • 应用内购买：购买交易由 Google Play 或 Apple App Store 处理，受 Google 和 Apple 各自的隐私政策约束
  • 法律要求：根据法律法规、法律程序或政府部门的强制性要求
  • 用户同意：在获得您明确同意后

我们不会出于广告目的而共享、出售或分发您的个人信息。

This Privacy Policy describes how ToolKit ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

📋1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum information necessary to provide its core features. We do not collect, upload, or store your personal identification information (such as name, email address, phone number, etc.).

🔧2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Audio/Video Processing: Your selected media files are processed locally on your device. You decide whether to save or delete the results
  • Free Trial Management: Device information is used to generate a device fingerprint for managing 7 free trial uses and preventing tampering
  • Purchase Verification: We verify your purchase status through Google Play Billing or Apple StoreKit to determine premium feature access
  • Data Security: AES-256-CBC encryption protects locally stored sensitive data against tampering and unauthorized access

🔒3. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data very seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Local Storage: All data (including usage counts, purchase status) is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to any remote server
  • AES-256-CBC Encryption: Sensitive data (free trial count, purchase status) is encrypted using AES-256-CBC algorithm with a randomly generated IV for each encryption
  • Device-Bound Key: The encryption key is derived from your device's hardware fingerprint (SHA-256), bound to the device, and cannot be decrypted on other devices
  • HMAC-SHA256 Integrity Check: Encrypted data includes an integrity verification code that detects any tampering; tampered data is reset rather than misinterpreted
  • Temp File Cleanup: Temporary files generated during processing are stored in the system temp directory and can be manually cleaned by you

🤝4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to any third party. Exceptions include:

  • In-App Purchases: Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play or Apple App Store and are subject to Google's and Apple's respective privacy policies
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, legal process, or government authority
  • User Consent: With your explicit consent

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information for advertising purposes.

⏱️5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Media Files: Used temporarily during processing only; no copies are retained after processing is complete
  • Usage Count & Purchase Status: Stored locally on your device; uninstalling the App will automatically delete all data
  • Temporary Files: Stored in the system temp directory; can be manually cleaned or automatically reclaimed by the system
  • You may delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App
